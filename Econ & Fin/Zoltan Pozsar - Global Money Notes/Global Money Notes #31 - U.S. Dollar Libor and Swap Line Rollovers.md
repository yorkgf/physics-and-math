# Global Money Notes #31: U.S. Dollar Libor and Swap Line Rollovers
**Zoltan Pozsar**  
**Date**: June 11, 2020  
**Credit Suisse**

---

## 核心命题

> The market's concerns over the upcoming swap line maturities are overdone: in our view, the maturities this month will pass without any major market impact.
> 
> The current state of dollar funding markets reminds us of a bathtub full of toys, with the faucet still running and the drain at the bottom open: there is some drama developing at the bottom, as a whirlpool coming out of nowhere starts to suck some little toys in – balances in the Treasury general account (TGA) reach a record $1.5 trillion and the o/n fed funds rate ticks up a massive basis point.
> 
> The water level in the tub – the total amount of reserves in the banking system – begins to fall. But then the use of the o/n repo facility suddenly begins to rise – the faucet is being turned up on demand, and that's a good thing. In this context, we don't understand why the market is worried about the use of the repo facility: you got the standing facility you were asking for, so just use it and don't worry; and you got something even cooler – a standing dollar swap line at a low rate.
> 
> With these facilities, bill supply, rising TGA balances and swap line rollovers don't matter: if the water level drops, the faucet will be turned up – on demand, at a fixed price.

---

## 市场担忧什么

三月底从美联储swap line借出的$350亿将在六月底到期，市场担心：

- 这些到期后，银行转回市场融资 → 推高Libor-OIS spread → 重新收紧融资条件

Pozsar说：**过度担忧了**。

## 核心分析

### 两种情况，都没问题

1. **如果外国银行依然需要美元** → 会直接roll over swap line贷款 → 美联储已经有工具，固定价格OIS+25bps → 市场不会感受到压力
2. **如果外国银行不需要了** → 还钱给美联储 → 这些美元原本在外国央行，现在放出来到银行间市场 → 一级交易商可以直接从美联储o/n repo facility拿到 → 还是不会有压力，因为standing repo facility按需供应，固定价格

> "What goes down, must come up... at a fixed price, on demand."

### BTPs是最大赢家

- 互换额度让美元变成可互换：外国央行可以把外国主权债（比如意大利BTPs）抵押给美联储拿到美元 → 外国银行可以把美元借给本地非银行 → BTPs获得流动性支持
- 因此，BTPs是当前"战争金融"体系下最大受益者

---

## 最终结论

> "the Fed's standing liquidity facilities will ensure that what drains from the tub, will surely flow back in – at a fixed price, on demand."

swap line到期根本不是问题，现有工具足够应对。
