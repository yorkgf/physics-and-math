# Global Money Notes #11: One Hundred Million Tons of Reserves
**Zoltan Pozsar**  
**Date**: December 6, 2017  
**Credit Suisse**

---

## 核心命题

> The three-month dollar-yen basis has narrowed significantly since mid-September, in line with our long-held view that the widening we saw over the summer would reverse over the fall because of one simple fact – the Federal Reserve’s balance sheet runoff mechanically reduces the volume of Treasury collateral that dealers hold, which mechanically reduces the amount of dollar liquidity that dealers can provide to cross-currency basis arbitrageurs... and when the runoff stops, basis will narrow.
> 
> A quick review of the mechanics of how runoff affects the basis will tell you that when the Fed’s balance sheet shrinks by $1 trillion, the supply of Treasuries to the market increases by $1 trillion... and if those $1 trillion of extra Treasuries have to be absorbed by dealer balance sheets... that can only mean one thing... one hundred million tons of extra reserves will be needed to hold that extra collateral... which means that when the Fed stops shrinking, the basis will stop widening... the question is not if... it's when... and the when is June 2018... that's when the FOMC will pause... and that's when the basis will find its new equilibrium level... between -30 and -40 bps... wider than the pre-QE4 normal... because post-Basel III, wider bases are the new normal.

---

## 机械传导逻辑

| 美联储操作 | 对国债供给 | 对dealer资产负债表 | 对准备金 | 对基差 |
|------------|--------------|---------------------|------------|----------|
| QE 买国债 | 减少供给 → dealer不需要持有 → 需要更少准备金 | 增加准备金 | 基差收窄（变得不那么负） |
| 缩table 停止买国债 | 增加供给 → dealer必须持有 → 需要更多准备金 | 减少准备金 | 基差走阔（变得更负） |

**关键结论**：当美联储停止缩table，基差就会停止走阔，找到新均衡。

> 「每减少1万亿美元准备金 → 基差走阔约50bps」

---

## 新均衡在哪里

**后巴塞尔III时代，更宽的基差就是新常态**

- 停止缩表后，$/¥ 基差新均衡大概在 **-30 ~ -40 bps**
- 比QE4之前（缩table之前）更宽，这是结构性的，不是周期性的，因为巴塞尔III约束了银行资产负债表
