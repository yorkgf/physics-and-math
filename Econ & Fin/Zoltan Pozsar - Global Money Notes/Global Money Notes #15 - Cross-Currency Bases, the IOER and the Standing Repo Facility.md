# Global Money Notes #15: Cross-Currency Bases, the IOER and the Standing Repo Facility
**Zoltan Pozsar**  
**Date**: August 1, 2018  
**Credit Suisse**

---

## 核心命题

> Cross-currency bases have widened after the recent Fed rate hike... the widening is directly attributable to the increase in IOER... wider bases are a feature, not a bug... and QE4 is still coming... the reason QE4 is coming is that it is the only way to increase the supply of reserves and increase the capacity of the U.S. banking system to absorb more collateral... and make room on bank balance sheets for more cross-currency basis arbitrage... and narrow cross-currency bases... the standing repo facility can't do it... only QE4 can.

---

## IOER increase为什么会让基差走阔

- IOER提高 → 银行持有准备金获得更高收益 → 银行更愿意持有准备金，而不是把准备金拿去做FX swap arbitrage
- 因此，arbitrage空间减少 → 基差走阔
- 这不是bug，是feature：银行本来就需要准备金满足监管要求，IOER提高自然增加持有准备金的吸引力，减少套利空间

## 为什么只有QE4能解决，standing repo facility不行

- standing repo facility 解决的是**日内流动性**问题 → 提供准备金应对日内抵押品波动
- 问题是**存量**问题：需要增加准备金存量，才能让银行有空间吸收更多国债，做更多arbitrage
- standing repo facility 解决流量，不解决存量 → 只有QE4能增加准备金存量

因此，standing repo facility 不可能替代 QE4。

## 结论

- IOER提高 → 基差走阔 → 这是feature不是bug
- 只有QE4能增加准备金存量 → 解决根本问题
- QE4 仍然在来的路上
