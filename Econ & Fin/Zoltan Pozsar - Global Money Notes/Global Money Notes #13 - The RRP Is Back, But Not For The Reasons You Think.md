# Global Money Notes #13: The RRP Is Back, But Not For The Reasons You Think
**Zoltan Pozsar**  
**Date**: April 18, 2018  
**Credit Suisse**

---

## 核心命题

> The o/n RRP facility has seen its usage increase from basically zero to close to $200 billion over the past three weeks... the increase in usage is not driven by the technical factors that most market commentary focus on... it's driven by prime money funds increasing their lending to foreign banks in the tri-party repo market, against the collateral of Treasuries... why are they doing this? because the liquidity coverage ratio (LCR) rules require that banks have enough HQLA... and when foreign banks buy Treasuries from the Fed's balance sheet runoff, they need to finance them... and they need to do that with secured borrowing... and prime money funds are the only game in town... which means that the runoff of the Fed's balance sheet is going better than expected... balance sheet adjustment is ongoing... it's working... in the "shadow"...
> 
> We expect the increase in RRP usage to continue through the end of the runoff... when the runoff ends, RRP usage will go back to zero... don't be scared that something is broken... everything is working as should be...

---

## 为什么RRP usage上升

市场主流说法：因为准备金增加了，所以货币基金用RRP。Pozsar说：**错**。

真实原因：

- 美联储缩table → 外国银行买了美联储放出来的国债
- 外国银行需要给这些国债融资 → 需要secured borrowing via repo
- 货币基金是repo市场最大贷款人 → 货币基金贷出越多 → 最后还是要存RRP，因为清算之后会剩现金

所以，**RRP usage上升，说明缩table进行得很顺利，不是坏事**。

## 结论

- RRP usage上升是缩table过程中正常的技术调整，不是市场出问题了
- 缩table完成后，RRP usage会回到零
- 不用担心，一切正常，资产负债表调整正在进行中
