# Global Money Notes #12: Cross-Currency Bases, Credit Spreads and QE4
**Zoltan Pozsar**  
**Date**: February 14, 2018  
**Credit Suisse**

---

## 核心命题

> The cross-currency basis of the yen is 25 bps tighter than it was this summer, and tighter than we expected, in line with our view that the basis would tighten as the probability of QE4 increased. But the tightening of the basis has come alongside a widening of credit spreads, not the narrowing that we would have expected if the tightening of the basis was coming from more reserves...
> 
> The widening of credit spreads has multiple causes... and the most important of them is the realization that the amount of excess reserves is what it is... and what it isn't... it isn't excess... So when we write QE4 the implication is that the Fed will push the amount of reserves where the basis trades where it should... closer to  -25 bps than to the -50 bps that we have today... but credit spreads should narrow after that... don't confuse the two... and don't confuse the widening of credit spreads with recession risk... this is balance sheet arithmetic... not macroeconomics.

---

## 核心机制

当美联储缩table减少准备金 → 基差走阔 → 如果QE4增加准备金 → 基差收窄。

但当前信用价差走阔，这不是因为衰退风险，而是因为：

- 银行资产负债表吸收额外国债 → 需要占用额外准备金 → 准备金稀缺 → 信用价差走阔
- 这是**资产负债表算术（balance sheet arithmetic）**，不是宏观经济衰退风险

因此，QE4不仅会让跨货币基差收窄，也会让信用价差收窄。

> "don't confuse the widening of credit spreads with recession risk... this is balance sheet arithmetic... not macroeconomics."

---

## 结论

- 基差收窄了，但信用价差走阔了
- 这是准备金稀缺导致的，不是经济衰退
- QE4会同时解决两个问题：基差收窄 + 信用价差收窄
