# Global Money Notes #28: Lombard Street and Pandemics
**Zoltan Pozsar**  
**Date**: March 17, 2020  
**Credit Suisse**

---

## 核心命题

> All segments of funding markets – secured, unsecured and FX swaps – continue to show growing signs of stress. The Fed may have to do more still.
> 
> In the U.S., we watched, but didn't feel the funding impact of large banks in other countries being asked to help their economies. Now that U.S. banks are asked to do the same, dollar funding markets are starting to feel the impact.
> 
> As U.S. banks increase their lending to the real economy as corporations draw on credit lines and banks lend more to households and firms, lending will consume more balance sheet and risk capital, and that will leave less room for market making and arbitrage, which under current circumstances are "luxury".
> 
> As banks are pulling back from market making, the Fed and other central banks need to assume the role of dealer of last resort...

---

## 当前问题

- 美联储已经降息，开启了流动性工具，但流动性并没有传导到每个角落
-  unsecured、secured、FX swap 各个市场都还有压力
- 问题：企业drawdown信贷额度 → 银行资产负债表被实体经济贷款占满 → 没有空间做市套利 → 做市功能失效

因此，美联储必须成为**Dealer of Last Resort**，而不仅仅是Lender of Last Resort。

> [[Dealer of Last Resort (最后交易商)]]
> [[Lender of Last Resort (最后贷款人)]]

## 需要做什么？

### 1. CP和CD市场需要买主

现在：

- 股权下跌 → 证券出借人现金抵押再投资账户现金回流 → 现金从CD/CP市场流向short end国债
- prime基金持续流出，流向政府基金 → 需求端减少
- 企业发行人发行量增加 → 供给端增加

所以问题：私人买家没了，美联储必须买。

但是美联储不应该通过CPFF，定价太高。正确做法：

- 美联储和财政部合作，财政部提供first-loss buffer
- 美联储直接买CP，定价在OIS+25bps，和其他工具一致
- 财政部出本金损失缓冲，美联储只承担流动性风险

这样可以立即生效，不需要长时间的机构准入流程。

### 2. 美元互换额度需要改进

- 当前互换额度只给G7央行，只每周操作一次，只给银行，不对非银行
- 问题：
  - 需要**daily**操作，不是每周
  - 需要扩展到更多央行，不仅仅G7（斯堪的纳维亚、东南亚、澳大利亚、南美都需要）
  - 需要允许央行再放贷给**非银行机构**（保险公司、资管），不仅仅给银行

因为后危机时代，非银行机构（寿险、资管）在FX swap市场已经超过银行，是边际需求方。只给银行不对。

> [[美元互换额度 (FX Swap Lines)]]

## 结论

> "When the sneeze gets into the plumbing of the dollar funding markets, the Fed needs to backstop not just the core of the system, but the periphery as well... It's time to lean in... It's time to become dealer of last resort."

---

## 核心论点：银行给实体经济放贷 → 挤占做市空间

> "As banks hoard the highest form of liquidity – reserves – the periphery will come knocking for liquidity. Now's not the time to end QE. It's time to lean in..."

当实体经济需要贷款，银行必须满足，这会占用银行资产负债表空间，做市和套利就没空间了 → 央行必须接盘做市功能。
