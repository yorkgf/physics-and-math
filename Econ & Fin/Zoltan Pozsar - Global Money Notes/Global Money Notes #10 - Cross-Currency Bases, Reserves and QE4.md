# Global Money Notes #10: Cross-Currency Bases, Reserves and QE4
**Zoltan Pozsar**  
**Date**: October 11, 2017  
**Credit Suisse**

---

## 核心命题

> Cross-currency bases widened out over the summer, led by a combination of: (1) the “reversal trade” in the cross-currency basis book; (2) large balance sheet expansions by the ECB and the BoJ, which are increasing the volume of dollar assets that need to be hedged; and (3) balance sheet constraints on dealers, where the $/yen basis is trading wider today than it was during the 2008 crisis.
> 
> In this note we answer two questions about the recent widening of cross-currency bases: who bears the brunt of the widening, and where do we go from here? The answer to the first question is Japan’s regional banks. The answer to the second question: QE4 is coming.

---

## 核心论证

### 三个驱动widening

1. **Reversal trade** → 缩table导致抽走准备金 → 美国银行没有多余资产负债表空间 → 价差走阔（widen）
2. **ECB和BoJ继续QE** → 欧洲日本投资者要买更多美元资产 → 需要对冲 → 需求增加 → 价差走阔
3. **资产负债表约束** →  dealer受到SLR约束 → 没法套利 → 价差无法收敛

结果：$/¥ 基差比 **2008年金融危机还宽**！

> [[跨货币基差 (cross-currency basis)]]
> [[SLR (补充杠杆率)]]

---

### 谁最受伤？

**日本区域银行**：

- 日本区域银行（regional banks）买了大量美元资产 → 需要对冲 → 对冲成本越来越高
- 他们买的都是长期投资级债券 → 不能随便卖 → 必须持有承受对冲成本侵蚀净息差
- 如果继续，总有一天他们会被迫出售美元资产 → 踩踏风险

### 为什么QE4会来

**美联储缩table → 抽走准备金 → 美国银行资产负债表更紧张 → 基差走阔。**

解决方案：**QE4**，就是美联储停止缩table，重新扩表释放准备金 → 给银行资产负债表空间 → 让他们做套利 → 基差收窄。

---

## 结论

> "Bases are wider today than they were during the 2008 crisis – that says it all. Bases don’t lie. Reserves are scarce, and QE4 is coming."
