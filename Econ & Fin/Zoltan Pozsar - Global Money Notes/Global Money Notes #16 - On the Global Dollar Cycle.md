# Global Money Notes #16: On the Global Dollar Cycle
**Zoltan Pozsar**  
**Date**: October 3, 2018  
**Credit Suisse**

---

## 核心命题

> On one side we have the ECB and the BoJ doing QE and on the other side we have the Fed doing QT... balance sheet normalization. QE by the G4 central banks lowers term premia... it steepens the yield curve for the U.S. it creates excess demand for long-end U.S. Treasuries, and it delays the inversion... QT creates a shortage of reserves... higher dollar LIBOR-OIS and cross-currency bases... higher short-end rates... which in turn steepens the dollar money curve...
> 
> The global dollar cycle is all about steepening... it's about the short end steepening and the long end flattening... the curve flattens to inversion... inversion kills the expansion... this is how expansions die... with a little help from the global dollar cycle...

---

## 全球美元周期的结构

全球美元周期有两个方面：

1. **ECB/BoJ QE → 降低本地期限溢价 → 投资者要买更多高收益美元长债 → 压低美国长端收益率 →  flatten 曲线前端后端

2. **美联储 QT（缩table）→ 抽走准备金 → 推高美元短端利率（LIBOR-OIS, cross-currency basis）→  steepen 短端

所以，**全球美元周期本质就是让曲线从短端 steepen，长端 flatten，最终走向 inversion，杀死扩张**。

## 结论

全球美元周期推动收益率曲线走向inversion，inversion预测衰退，所以全球美元周期杀死了扩张。

> "This is how expansions die... with a little help from the global dollar cycle..."
