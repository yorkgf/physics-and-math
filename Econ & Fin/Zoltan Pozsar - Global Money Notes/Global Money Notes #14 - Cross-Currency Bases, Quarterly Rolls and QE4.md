# Global Money Notes #14: Cross-Currency Bases, Quarterly Rolls and QE4
**Zoltan Pozsar**  
**Date**: June 19, 2018  
**Credit Suisse**

---

## 核心命题

> Cross-currency bases have widened into quarter-end, but the widening has been less than we expected... what should we make of it? One view is that the widening is all about "window dressing" – foreign banks shrinking their balance sheet on quarter-end... and that's it... we disagree... we think that the persistent widening of bases through multiple quarter-end rolls is consistent with our long-held view that we're getting closer to QE4... each roll increases the odds that you're getting closer to the edge... the edge is when the arbitrage that keeps bases stable at -25 to -30 bps can't absorb more... the next large increase in the size of the ECB's and the BoJ's balance sheets will push us over the edge... and that's when we get QE4... it's just a matter of time...

---

## 核心论证

每个季度末：

- 外国银行要window dressing → 缩减资产负债表 → 减少做FX swap arbitrage → 基差走阔
- 下个季度初，资产负债表扩张 → 基差收窄

但最近几个季度：

- 走阔的幅度越来越大
- 收窄的幅度越来越小
- 低点越来越低

这说明：资产负债表空间越来越小 → 越来越接近临界点，再往下就要QE4了。

### 背后逻辑

- ECB和BoJ继续QE → 欧元区日本投资者要买更多高收益美元资产 → 需要对冲 → 对FX swap美元需求越来越大
- 美国银行资产负债表空间有限 → 受SLR约束，不能无限扩张
- 每次季度roll，需求多一点，空间少一点 → 基差越来越宽
- 临界点到了 → 美联储必须QE4释放准备金 → 给美国银行空间

## 结论

每个季度滚动，基差最低点越来越低 → 越来越接近临界点，QE4越来越近，就是时间问题。
