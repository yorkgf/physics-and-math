# Unit 6: Energy and Momentum of Rotating Systems - In-Depth Concepts

## Key Concepts

- [[Rotational Kinetic Energy]] - Combined translational + rotational kinetic energy
- [[Rotational Form of Newton's Second Law]] - Fundamental equation of rotational dynamics
- [[Conservation of Rotational Energy]] - Energy conservation including rotational kinetic energy
- [[Rolling Motion]] - Pure rolling conditions, acceleration formulas
- [[Satellite Orbital Motion]] - Application: gravity provides centripetal force

## Derivation Map

```
[[Moment of Inertia]] → [[Rotational Kinetic Energy]] K = ½Iω²
                             → [[Rotational Form of Newton's Second Law]] Στ = Iα
                             → [[Conservation of Rotational Energy]] E = Ktrans + Krot + U
                             → [[Rolling Motion]] K = ½mv² + ½Iω², solve with translational + rotational equations

[[Universal Gravitation]] → [[Satellite Orbital Motion]] v = √(GM/r)
```
